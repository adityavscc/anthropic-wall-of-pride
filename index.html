Anthropic drew a line. No mass surveillance. No autonomous weapons without human oversight. The Pentagon demanded they erase it. They refused. The world responded.

Dario Amodei, CEO of Anthropic

What happened: The Pentagon demanded Anthropic remove two safety guardrails — the ban on AI-driven mass domestic surveillance and the requirement for human oversight on autonomous weapons. Anthropic refused. They were designated a "supply chain risk," and President Trump ordered all federal agencies to stop using their technology. 330+ employees across Google and OpenAI signed an open letter in solidarity. Chalk messages of gratitude covered the sidewalks outside Anthropic's San Francisco headquarters.
